===== Africa Central Information =====

Africa Central is one of three VATSSA Flight Service Stations serving the airspaces of [[country:angola:home|Angola]] ([[airspace:fnan:home|FNAN]]), [[country:burundi:home|Burundi]] ([[airspace:hbba:home|HBBA]]), [[country:congo-dr:home|Dem. Rep. of Congo]] ([[airspace:fzza:home|FZZA]]), [[country:kenya:home|Kenya]] ([[airspace:hkna:home|HKNA]]), [[country:malawi:home|Malawi]] ([[airspace:fwll:home|FWLL]]), [[country:rwanda:home|Rwanda]] ([[airspace:hryr:home|HRYR]]), [[country:st-helena:home|Saint Helena]] ([[airport:fhsh:home|FHSH]]), [[country:seychelles:home|Seychelles]] ([[airspace:fsss:home|FSSS]]), [[country:tanzania:home|Tanzania]] ([[airspace:htdc:home|HTDC]]), [[country:uganda:home|Uganda]] ([[airspace:huec:home|HUEC]]) and [[country:zambia:home|Zambia]] ([[airspace:flfi:home|FLFI]]).
